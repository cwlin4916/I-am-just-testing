What is this page about?

This page is about applications of technology to adressing various questions relating nature, see it's role in SDG goals. I stole the following pictorial schema from the latter paper. For AI I would mean briefly any technology that has the ability of:

  • Perception - audio,visual,textual, i.e. face recognintion.
  • Descision making - medical diagnosis.
  • Prediction,
    • Hurricane forecast,
    • PrevisIA, which predicts areas in Amazon rainforest prone to deforestation.
  • Extraction and pattern recognintion

Potential problems of applications

There are many facets to consider in the employment of AI for SDGs.

  • Unequal distribution - do small farmers get the technology?
  • Value misalignment.
    • How it is designed.
    • How it is trained. Learning on regular news can induce societal bias.
    A related field of study is thus explainable AI. I should have a read at the references in blog posts.
  • lack of interpretability

What is Conservation?

Conservation includes maintaining

  • diversity of species.
  • genes
  • ecosystems
  • fucntions of the envorinment

I take this different to preservation. To start helping with habitat we should understand how species interact with ecosystems. The following are some areas, stolen from NWF.

Forest degradation

This is different to deforestation. A good reference is Simula. The process can be

  • natural such as fire, storm or disease.
  • human induced, due to human logging.
It is thus broadly defined as its lack of capacity to provided goods and services.
