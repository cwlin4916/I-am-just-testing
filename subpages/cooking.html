滷肉飯. Some from southern Taiwan refer this as 肉燥飯. In most cases you would see these as minced pork stewed in soya sauce. Some from northern Taiwan also refer this as 爌肉飯, but this usually refers to larger pieces of pork meat. The ingredients are simple.

    • Instructions:
    1. Brown the pork. If you see browning sugar, shake the pan.
    2. Now put anything you have in your pot.
    3. Sauce.
